Processing tips
Schedule earlyMost lockers book out weeks to months ahead — call as soon as you know your sell date.
Hanging weightProcessing priced on hanging weight (after slaughter) — typically 60–65% of live weight
Cut sheetProvide a cut sheet specifying steaks, roasts, and ground beef ratios before drop-off
USDA inspectFor retail sales, processor must be USDA-inspected — Herda's in Faribault qualifies
